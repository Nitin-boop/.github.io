About Me

Games have always been a huge part of my life. It's a place where I discovered a lot about myself.

From the fear of missing out when your party decides to set out for an adventure to the feeling of emptiness when you complete the game you loved playing, games invoke a range of emotions that you never knew existed. Games have the power to transend reality.

Now, is there any other better way to show your imaginations? Game development is a very powerful tool and the responsibility lies in the hands of the developers to get in touch with the emotions of the players. I want players to feel the emotions I felt through my life. I believe in the potential of interactive storytelling and aim to connect with players through my creations. Although I am experimenting at the moment, I want to make sure that each game I develop inspires creativity, resilience and a sense of wonder. Whether it's exploring a fantasy world, solving intricate puzzles or overcoming obstacles, I want players to feel a connection to the stories and characters I've crafted.

As part of my freelance work, I took on the challenge of conceptualizing, designing, and developing Bowled.io. Emotion Escapade
Unity Narrative Emotional Exploration Platformer

Emotion Escapade is a narrative journey through the landscape of emotions in a side-scrolling platformer. Players become the character narrating his way through life representing a wide range of emotions like happiness, sadness and anger, and when the player equips these emotions, he gets a unique ability. The levels also are a symbolic representation of emotional challenges, offering the experience mirroring the ups and downs of real life emotions.

Flora Chase
Custome Game Engine C++ Endless Runner

“Flora Chase” is an endless runner game set in a world of flora. Players must sprint through landscapes, avoiding trees and collecting diamonds along the way. The goal is to survive and score as high as possible in this thrilling chase through nature’s wonders.
